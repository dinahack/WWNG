Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get involved?

You can volunteer, donate, attend events, or simply join our newsletter to stay updated about community activities.

Do you accept donations?

Yes! We accept donations through PayPal, Canada Helps, and bank transfers. Visit our donations page for more details.

How often do you hold events?

We host various events throughout the year. Check our Events page or subscribe to our newsletter for updates.

What's your response time?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours.
