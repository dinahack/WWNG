About Westminster Woods

Our Story, Our Mission, Our Community

Our Mission

Westminster Woods is dedicated to building a vibrant, inclusive community where neighbors support one another. We work to keep our neighbourhood safe, our families nourished, and our community empowered.

Our Vision

We envision a neighbourhood where every resident feels valued, supported, and connected. A place where community members work together to create positive change and lasting friendships.

Our Core Values

Community

We believe in the power of neighbors coming together to support one another.

Inclusivity

Everyone is welcome. We celebrate diversity and ensure all voices are heard.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas and creative solutions to community challenges.

Empowerment

We empower community members to take action and make a difference.

Safety

Creating and maintaining a safe, welcoming environment for all residents.

Compassion

We care for one another and respond with kindness and understanding.

Our Story

Westminster Woods began as a grassroots initiative by a group of passionate neighbors who believed in the power of community. Founded on the principle that strong neighborhoods are built on meaningful connections and mutual support, our organization has grown to become a vital hub for community engagement and support.

Over the years, we've organized countless events, supported families and individuals in need, and created a vibrant network of neighbors who genuinely care about one another. From our humble beginnings, Westminster Woods has evolved into a trusted community organization that serves as a beacon of hope and connection for all residents.

Today, we continue our mission with renewed dedication, working to address the evolving needs of our community while staying true to our founding values of compassion, inclusivity, and empowerment.
